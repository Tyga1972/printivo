favorite food and how to prepare it

  • Amala
  • Ewedu
  • Gbegiri

Amala is a local Nigerian food made from yam after going through several process. How to make Amala goes as thus, pour a little quantity of water accordint to the size of amala you want to prepare wait till the water boil and add your yam flour to it , steer together on fire till its thick and ready for consumption

Ewedu is a local Nigeria delicacy cooked especially by the yoruba's, there are different ways to cook ewedu but it depends on how the consumers like it. i like my ewedu prepared well with melon locust beans. 1st step is adding a cup of water on fire and letting it boil for few minute and pouring your ewedu leaf to let it cooked for few minute after then you can add your melon and locust beans with a little salt then your ewedu soup is ready

Gbegiri is a common Nigerian soup that almost everyone like to eat. here is how to prepare gbegiri. get a white beans and cook for few minute without salt,after its soften to your satisfaction pour it in a blender or grinder to grind smoothly , after then you can pour it on fire and add your palm oil blended crayfish salt ETC. let it cook for few minute then your gbegiri is done.
